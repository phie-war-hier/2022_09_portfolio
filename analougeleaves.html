analouge leaves

photography

analouge leaves is a two-part photo project, created in the winter semester 2021/2022 at the faculty of design mannheim. part one consists of a series photographed with a self-made camera obscura. the second part was shot with a reflex camera converted to a digital camera obscura. the two series are linked by the theme of leaving.
