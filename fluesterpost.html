flüsterpost

experimental

research with aesthetic methods: what is nature? do we need nature? how is the relationship between human and nature and why is it out of balance? more questions than answers are the result, which we compiled in a brochure.

"flüsterpost" is an experimental system that anna kaufmann, michelle michewicz and sophie humbert developed in the winter semester 21/22 of communication design at the faculty of design mannheim. the class was taught by prof. dr. klenk.
