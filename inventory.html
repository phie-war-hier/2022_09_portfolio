editorial

information design – diagrams, whether circles or bars, everyone has seen them before. can knowledge and information be communicated wrong? can design affect information? yes, often this happens unconsciously. information design has the curse or blessing to shape opinions, change and form a society. even the dirtiest thing can look clean – figuratively, but also literally.

a project in the summer semester 2021 at the faculty of design mannheim.
