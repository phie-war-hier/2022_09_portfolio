planta v1

experimental

planta v1 was created in the summer semester of 2022 in the course interactive media, as a group project by teresa hoffmann, paco hardt and sophie humbert. through interaction such as touching or watering the plants, visuals and music can be controlled.

the technology behind it is composed of an arduino kit and program, a processing sketch, an ableton set, a lot of cable and a controllable led stripe also called neopixels.

in the exhibition captcha 2022 at the mannheimer kunstverein the installation planta v1 was set up and exhibited.
